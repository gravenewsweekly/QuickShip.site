Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the prices shown?

Our prices are updated weekly from official courier websites. For exact rates, please book through our platform.

Is there any hidden charge?

No, we show the final price including all charges. What you see is what you pay.

How do I track my shipment?

After booking, you'll receive a tracking link via email. You can also use the tracking page of the respective courier.
